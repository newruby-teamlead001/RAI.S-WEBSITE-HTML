About New Ruby

New Ruby is a youth-led initiative founded with the goal of inspiring and empowering students and curious minds across Nepal and beyond.

Our Story

Started as a simple idea in 2018, New Ruby has grown into a platform where creativity, knowledge, and collaboration meet. We believe that young people can drive meaningful change when given the right space to explore, express, and engage.

What We Do

  • Host community projects and events
  • Support youth-led creativity and innovation
